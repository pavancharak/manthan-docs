<h1>System</h1>

<p>
Manthan defines how decisions are made, validated, and enforced in software systems.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>The Problem</h2>

<p>
AI systems operate in probabilistic generation.
</p>

<ul>
<li>Same input → different outputs</li>
<li>No traceability</li>
<li>No enforcement</li>
</ul>

<p>
<strong>This makes them unreliable for decisions.</strong>
</p>

<img src="../assets/problem.png" alt="Problem" style="width:100%; border-radius:8px; margin:20px 0;">

<hr>

<h2>Generation vs Decision</h2>

<p>
AI operates in <strong>generation mode</strong>.<br>
Manthan operates in <strong>decision mode</strong>.
</p>

<ul>
<li>Generation → possibilities</li>
<li>Decision → outcomes</li>
</ul>

<p>
Prompts generate.<br>
Contracts decide.
</p>

<img src="../assets/shift.png" alt="Shift" style="width:100%; border-radius:8px; margin:20px 0;">

<hr>

<h2>Decision Boundary</h2>

<p>
AI is useful for ambiguity.<br>
Decisions require certainty.
</p>

<div style="display:flex; gap:40px; flex-wrap:wrap;">

<div>
<p><strong>AI (Generation)</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Interpretation</li>
<li>Exploration</li>
<li>Ambiguity</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div>
<p><strong>Decision Systems</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Determinism</li>
<li>Enforcement</li>
<li>Guarantees</li>
</ul>
</div>

</div>

<p>
Crossing this boundary turns insight into decision.
</p>

<img src="../assets/decision-boundary.png" alt="Decision Boundary" style="width:100%; border-radius:8px; margin:20px 0;">

<hr>

<h2>System Model</h2>

<p>
A Manthan system follows a deterministic structure:
</p>

<ul>
<li>Intent → defined as contracts</li>
<li>Contracts → define decisions</li>
<li>Decisions → executed deterministically</li>
<li>Execution → enforced by the system</li>
</ul>

<img src="../assets/architecture.png" alt="System Model" style="width:100%; border-radius:8px; margin:20px 0;">

<hr>

<h2>Contracts</h2>

<p>
Contracts define decisions explicitly.
</p>

<ul>
<li>Versioned</li>
<li>Immutable</li>
<li>Deterministic</li>
</ul>

<p>
They are the source of truth.
</p>

<p>
Decisions are not inferred.<br>
<strong>They are specified.</strong>
</p>

<img src="../assets/contracts.png" alt="Contracts" style="width:100%; border-radius:8px; margin:20px 0;">

<hr>

<h2>Intelligence Layer</h2>

<p>
Decisions are made first.
</p>

<ul>
<li>Explanation</li>
<li>Context</li>
<li>Scoring</li>
</ul>

<p>
This layer does not influence decisions.<br>
<strong>It explains decisions — it never makes them.</strong>
</p>

<img src="../assets/intelligence.png" alt="Intelligence" style="width:100%; border-radius:8px; margin:20px 0;">

<hr>

<h2>Outcome</h2>

<p><strong>Without deterministic decisions:</strong></p>

<ul>
<li>Inconsistent outcomes</li>
<li>No traceability</li>
<li>No enforcement</li>
</ul>

<p><strong>With Manthan:</strong></p>

<ul>
<li>Consistent decisions</li>
<li>Full traceability</li>
<li>Enforced outcomes</li>
</ul>

<p>
<strong>Reliable systems require deterministic decisions.</strong>
</p>
