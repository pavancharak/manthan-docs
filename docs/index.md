<h1>Manthan</h1>

<p style="font-size:20px;"><strong>Decision Infrastructure for AI Systems</strong></p>

<p>
Manthan is infrastructure for deterministic, auditable decision-making in software systems.
</p>

<p>
AI systems today are fundamentally unreliable.
</p>

<p>
Same input produces different outputs.<br>
There is no traceability.<br>
There is no enforcement.
</p>

<p><strong>This makes them unsuitable for critical decision-making.</strong></p>

<hr>

<h2>The Shift</h2>

<p>
AI operates in generation mode.<br>
Manthan operates in decision mode.
</p>

<p>
Generation is not decision-making.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>What Manthan Focuses On</h2>

<ul>
<li>Deterministic decision systems</li>
<li>Auditable execution paths</li>
<li>Enforceable outcomes</li>
<li>Decision infrastructure as a primitive</li>
</ul>
