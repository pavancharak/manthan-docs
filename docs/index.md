<h1>Manthan</h1>

<p style="font-size:20px;"><strong>Decision Infrastructure for AI Systems</strong></p>

<p>
Manthan defines how decisions are made, enforced, and verified in software systems.
</p>

<p>
AI systems today operate in probabilistic generation.
</p>

<p>
Same input can produce different outputs.<br>
There is no traceability.<br>
There is no enforcement.
</p>

<p><strong>This makes them unfit for critical decision-making.</strong></p>

<hr>

<h2>The Shift</h2>

<p>
AI operates in <strong>generation mode</strong>.<br>
Manthan operates in <strong>decision mode</strong>.
</p>

<p>
Generation produces possibilities.<br>
Decision systems produce outcomes.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>What Manthan Enables</h2>

<ul>
<li>Deterministic decision execution</li>
<li>Traceable and auditable reasoning</li>
<li>Enforced outcomes, not suggestions</li>
<li>Decision infrastructure as a system primitive</li>
</ul>
