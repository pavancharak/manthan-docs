<h2 style="text-align:center;">Why I Built This</h2>



<p style="max-width:720px; margin:auto; line-height:1.8;">

I did not start by trying to build an AI system.



<br><br>



I started with a simpler observation:



<br><br>



<strong>

Software systems fail not because they cannot compute,

but because they cannot make correct decisions.

</strong>



<br><br>



Systems today are very good at computation.  

They are very good at processing inputs.  

They are even becoming good at generation.



<br><br>



But decision-making remains:

</p>



<ul style="max-width:720px; margin:auto; line-height:1.8;">

&#x20; <li>Inconsistent</li>

&#x20; <li>Non-traceable</li>

&#x20; <li>Non-enforceable</li>

</ul>



<br>



<p style="max-width:720px; margin:auto; line-height:1.8;">

As systems become more autonomous, this becomes a structural problem.



That is the gap Manthan is built to solve.

</p>
